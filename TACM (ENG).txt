#### Puzzle 2:


You have a balance scale and three weights. You are told that one of the weights is a different mass than the other two, but you are not told if it is heavier or lighter.


Using the balance scale only once, how can you guarantee you will identify the odd weight and determine whether it is heavier or lighter?
